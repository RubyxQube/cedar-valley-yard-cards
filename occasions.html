Every Celebration Has Its Moment

We set up yard cards for birthdays, graduations, welcome homes, new babies, retirements, and everything in between.

Birthdays

The Classic Birthday Yard Card

Whether they're turning 1 or 100, a birthday display stops everyone in the neighborhood. We bring the letters, the numbers, the decorative pieces - all of it. You tell us the age, the colors, and the name, and we handle the rest.

Popular options include balloon bouquets, themed character pieces, and confetti cutouts. Ask us when you book.

Graduations

Grad Season is Our Favorite

Eagle Mountain and Lehi seniors graduate in May and June - and their yards should show it. We do class years, school names, graduate names, and school colors. Nothing feels more like a big deal than pulling up to your house and seeing your name in 4-foot letters.

Book early in the spring. May and June dates go fast - sometimes 6-8 weeks out.

Book a Graduation
Welcome Home

Welcome Home is a Big Deal Here

Whether they're returning from a mission, deployment, or study abroad, the front yard display is the first thing they see. In Eagle Mountain and throughout Utah County, welcome home displays are a tradition - and we take that seriously.

We customize for missionary returns (mission name, homecoming date), military returns, and everything in between. The display is up before the car pulls into the neighborhood.
